How the Formula Works:
The tariff calculator estimates a fair tariff using the following expression:

Adjusted Tariff = ((PPI Gap + Development Gap) / 2) × (1 - Development Gap × δ) × 100 - Existing Tariff

Definitions:
  • PPI Gap: Reflects price level differences between countries (0 to 1 scale)
  • Development Gap: 1 - (Country GDP per Capita / U.S. GDP per Capita)
  • Delta (δ): Adjustable weight factor to scale development impact (default = 0.25)
  • Existing Tariff: Current average tariff already applied to the country
This approach balances price differences and income disparities to avoid over-penalizing less developed economies.
